Whenever scientific publications result from projects using BHM, we kindly ask for a citation to the paper describing the method:

O. Goulko, N. Prokof'ev, B. Svistunov
"Restoring a smooth function from its noisy integrals"
Phys. Rev. E 97, 053305 (2018)
https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevE.97.053305
eprint arXiv:1707.07625

and to the paper describing the code implementation:

O. Goulko, A. Gaenko, E. Gull, N. Prokof'ev, B. Svistunov
"Implementation of the Bin Hierarchy Method for restoring a smooth function from a sampled histogram", 
Computer Physics Communications 236, 205-213 (2019)
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cpc.2018.09.019
eprint arXiv:1711.04316
